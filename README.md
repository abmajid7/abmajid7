## 🏛️ Ranganathan's Five Laws — My Professional Compass

> Dr. S.R. Ranganathan's Five Laws are not just academic principles — they are the ethical foundation I bring to every library interaction.

```
  ① Books are for Use              — Resources exist to be accessed, not archived away.
  ② Every Book Its Reader          — Every piece of knowledge has someone who needs it.
  ③ Every Reader Their Book        — No one should leave a library empty-handed.
  ④ Save the Time of the Reader    — Good systems are acts of respect for people's time.
  ⑤ A Library is a Growing Organism — Evolve or become irrelevant.
```
